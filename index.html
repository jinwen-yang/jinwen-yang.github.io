Jinwen Yang

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at the University of Chicago, advised by Professor Haihao Lu. Previously, I obtained my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Fudan University.

My research interests are in optimization, with a particular focus on optimization algorithms tailored to modern hardware (like GPUs) and intended for practical applications. I am excited that our algorithms are now part of many optimization software platforms, including NVIDIA cuOpt and Gurobi.

Research Interests

Computation

Harnessing modern computational infrastructure such as GPUs

Methodology & Theory

Designing algorithms tailored for modern hardware, with rigorous theoretical guarantees

Applications

Solving practical problems in data science, operations research, and machine learning
